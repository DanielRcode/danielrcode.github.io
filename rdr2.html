GET NATURALIST BONUSES AND CELEBRATE THANKSGIVING ALL MONTH LONG IN RED DEAD ONLINE

3X RDO$ and XP for Selling Samples and in Naturalist Free Roam Events, Bonuses for Stealing The Ember of the East, and More

November 3rd, 2022

As autumn fades and the chill of winter draws near, Naturalists bundle up and venture forth into the wilderness in search of rare fauna. Selling Samples to conservationist Harriet Davenport will get you Triple RDO$ and XP, all month long.

In anticipation of the impending fallow and frigid winter months, the feasts of Thanksgiving are causing a spike in demand for animal products, resulting in Double Rewards on all Butcher Sales.

3X RDO$ AND XP ON WILD ANIMAL TAGGING AND WILDLIFE PHOTOGRAPHER FREE ROAM EVENTS

Team up with your fellow Naturalists and comb the landscape in search of beasts to sedate in Wild Animal Tagging or engage in the creative pursuit of capturing wildlife in repose in the Wildlife Photographer Free Roam Events, both paying out 3X RDO$ and XP for the next four weeks. Completing a round of Wildlife Photographer will also net you an orange Raccoon Hat.

Play Red Dead Online any week this month and receive a gift of 5 Blending Tonics and 5 Predator Bait to aid on your hunts and tracking expeditions in the wilderness. Turn in 2 Samples to Harriet any week this month and you will receive an Offer for 50% off an Established or Distinguished Naturalist Role Item and 200 rounds of Sedative Ammo.

FEATURED SERIES CALENDAR

Kick off this month's Featured Series with an assortment of Showdown Modes in Red Dead Online's southernmost state, then test your skill across all sorts of Hardcore variations. All Rewards and Offers will be delivered within 72 hours of completion.

  • November 1 – 7: Lemoyne Series - Craft a Coat and a Trinket at Gus’ Store to receive an orange pair of Grayshott Pants
  • November 8 – 14: Hardcore Mix - Complete an Animal Sighting Mission to earn the Blackgrave Coat dyed yellow and brown
  • November 15 – 21: Sport of Kings and Last Stand - Craft a Legendary Panther Coat and you’ll get a brown and red pair of Pelt Half Chaps
  • November 22 – 28: Hardcore Special Edition Modes - Naturalists above Rank 10 will get the orange and brown Kelley Boots this week
